Explorer Race

is an endless runner I developed solo in Unity for the Kenney Jam 2023 with the theme Exploration. In this game, the player goes up against the computer to see who can reach the end the fastest while having the best stat total, thus deciding once and for all who is the best at exploring.

Itch.io

Notable features include:

Procedural Generation

Both the environment and the obstacles are procedurally generated.
The environment has 3 tile variants, that were used to make the game feel less repetitive. These tiles are randomly selected with equal probability and are placed in the world as the player moves forward. At the same time, older tiles are removed and the back wall is moved up.
Then there are the "obstacles hurdles". Each hurdle can have 1, 2 or 3 obstacles in it, determined at random. The probability of having a lower number of obstacles is lower than having a higher number of obstacles. Finally, there are 6 different types of obstacles, and each affects the explorer's stats differently when they collide with it. The type of obstacles in a hurdle is also determined randomly.

Split Screen

Split screen was achieved by using 2 cameras, one for each explorer, and by using Unity's Cinemachine package to follow each explorer on their path. Then, the screen was divided by changing the cameras' viewport coordinates.

Enemy Explorer

At first, I thought I could implement a utility-based AI but by this stage, I was reaching the time limit of the jam, so I decided to just have the Enemy choose one of the 3 possible obstacles on the next hurdle at random.
